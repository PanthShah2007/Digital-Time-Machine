# ⏳ Digital Time Machine

**Travel Through the Evolution of Technology**

Digital Time Machine is an interactive web application that lets users explore the fascinating journey of technology through different decades. From the birth of computers and the internet to modern AI innovations, the website presents historical milestones in a visually engaging and immersive timeline.

## 🌐 Live Demo

**Website:** https://panthshah2007.github.io/Digital-Time-Machine/

---

## 📖 About

Technology has evolved at an incredible pace over the last century. Digital Time Machine brings together major innovations, breakthrough inventions, iconic devices, and significant events into one beautifully designed platform.

Whether you're a student, developer, or technology enthusiast, this project provides an engaging way to understand how the digital world has transformed over time.

---

## ✨ Features

* ⏳ Interactive chronological timeline
* 💻 Explore the evolution of computers and software
* 🌍 Discover major internet and technology milestones

* 🎨 Modern and responsive user interface

## 🎯 Purpose

The objective of this project is to make learning technology history more engaging through interactive design and storytelling. Instead of reading lengthy articles, users can visually travel through time and explore the evolution of the digital era in an intuitive way.
